About E-Waste Portal

Our mission is to promote safe, efficient, and sustainable e-waste disposal while raising awareness about its environmental impact.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team bridging the gap between households, organizations, and certified e-waste recyclers. We bring ease, accessibility, and accountability to e-waste management.

Every year, millions of devices become obsolete. We help ensure they are recycled or reused responsibly to protect our planet and reduce environmental harm.

What We Do

Our platform simplifies e-waste management by enabling you to:

  • Schedule convenient doorstep pickups.
  • Locate certified recycling facilities nearby.
  • Report improper disposal cases.
  • Access resources for recycling best practices.

We collaborate with government bodies and recyclers to ensure all electronic waste is processed responsibly and transparently.

Why E-Waste Recycling Matters

E-waste often contains both valuable resources and hazardous substances. Recycling helps us recover metals like gold, silver, and copper while preventing toxins from harming our ecosystems.

This practice also supports energy conservation, job creation, and a circular economy where waste becomes a resource.

Our Vision & Values

  • Environmental Sustainability: Creating greener systems for our planet.
  • Transparency: Clear accountability in every step of recycling.
  • Community Engagement: Empowering individuals to make eco-conscious choices.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas for better e-waste solutions.
